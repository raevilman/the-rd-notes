## What?
MEL is a no-nonsense personal expense tracker built for people who want to stay on top of their spending without the complexity of full-blown finance apps.

- Free
- Offline-first
- Cross-device sync via Google account
- No ads, no tracking, no analytics

## Why?
Most expense apps are either too complex or full of ads and upsells. I built MEL as a focused, privacy-first tool to log expenses quickly, see a monthly overview, and stay in sync across devices — nothing more.

## Features

**Offline-first** — your data is always available on device. Syncs automatically when back online via Firebase Firestore.

**Cross-device sync** — sign in with Google and your expenses follow you everywhere.

**Visual spending overview** — a bar chart shows monthly spending at a glance. Filter by item name or date range and the chart updates instantly.

**Powerful filters** — filter by item name (multi-select), set a custom date range, and review active filters as removable pills.

**Recurring expenses** — set up rent, subscriptions, or EMIs once. MEL auto-applies them so you never miss a regular payment.

**Quick edits** — tap any expense to edit name, amount, or date. Three-dot menu for edit and delete.

**Material 3 UI** — follows your system dark/light mode. Minimal chrome, maximum focus on data.
