## What?
Kowlt is a personalized learning web app built around a simple loop — **Read, Quiz, Retain**.

Pick any topic you're curious about, read focused AI-generated content, reinforce it with adaptive quizzes, and track your retention over time.

- Free tier available
- Works in the browser and installs as a PWA
- Community-explored trending topics
- No clutter — just the learning loop

## Why?
I wanted a focused way to explore topics deeply without getting lost in scattered tabs or bloated apps. Kowlt is built around the idea that curiosity is best served by a tight loop: read something focused, immediately quiz yourself on it, then revisit before it fades. No social feed, no distractions — just the loop.

## How it works

**Read** — Start a master topic. Kowlt generates focused content and lets you branch into subtopics that build momentum naturally.

**Quiz** — After reading, take adaptive quizzes that target the key ideas. Quizzes sharpen recall rather than just testing recognition.

**Retain** — Kowlt tracks your learning signals across topics and surfaces ones you should revisit before they fade.

## Features

- **Topic Library** — browse and pick from a growing set of topics or start any topic from scratch
- **Journey view** — visualise your learning path through a topic tree as you branch deeper
- **Trending topics** — see what the community is exploring
- **Badges** — account-level milestones that recognise your learning achievements across topics
- **Credits system** — content generation is credit-based; free tier included, upgrade for more
- **PWA** — install it on any device straight from the browser
